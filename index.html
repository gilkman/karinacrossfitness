About Karina

Karina Cross is an internationally recognized master trainer, body worker, motivational speaker, and fitness model. She's a 12-time National Champion in Power Lifting in her native country of Ecuador, holding national records in bench press (1996–2003) and deadlifting (1992–2003).

Karina specializes in strength training and conditioning, functional movement, weight loss, and athletic performance — and has a rare gift for working sensitively and effectively with clients across all walks of life: pregnancy, seniors, cancer patients, cardiac patients, spinal issues, joint replacement, and metabolic diseases.

If you're ready to transform not just your body but your entire relationship with fitness, Karina welcomes the opportunity to guide your journey.
